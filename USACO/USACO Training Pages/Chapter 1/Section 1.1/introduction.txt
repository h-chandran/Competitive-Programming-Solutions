The USA Computing Olympiad fosters pre-college computing through training, contests, and international competitions.

Specific goals of the USACO include:
 - Train USA team to win multiple gold medals at the next IOI.
 - Engage USA students in learning and growing to achieve the first goal
 - Conduct an eight day USA Olympiad for the best 15 USA students in order to identify   
   the final four USA representatives for international competition.
